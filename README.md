## üó∫Ô∏è The 12-Phase Roadmap
Based on the structured curriculum, this roadmap focuses on the transition from a "Model-Centric" to a "System-Centric" engineer.

### üß¨ Phase 1-4: Foundations & Deep Modeling
* **01. Math & Statistics:** The bedrock for evaluation and debugging systems.
* **02. DSA for AI Engineering:** Optimizing for high-performance and streaming data.
* **03. Machine Learning:** Focusing on ML as a decision-making system.
* **04. Deep Learning:** Understanding scaling laws and failure modes in neural networks.

### ‚ö° Phase 5-8: System Architecture & Data
* **05. NLP & GenAI:** Mastery of RAG pipelines, LLMs, and Agentic workflows.

* **08. AI System Design:** Designing stable orchestration around probabilistic components.

* **12. Strategic Updates:** Adapting to the fast-evolving AI landscape without "FOMO."
